About Us

We are a snowboarding club dedicated to building a supportive and inclusive community. No matter your skill level, you'll always find a suitable partner on the slopes.

Our philosophy revolves around fostering community and friendship while enjoying snowboarding. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, you'll find camaraderie in our club.

Supportive and Inclusive The Snowboarding Club is about meeting new people and having good, clean fun on the mountain. Our members range in ability so there'll always be someone at a similar ability to you who'll be happy to ride with you or just be around for a catch up at lunch or at the end of the day.
